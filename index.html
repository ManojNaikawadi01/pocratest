Officer–Farmer Governance Touchpoints — 4 Key Interactions
🏛️ 1. GKVS Formation — ग्रा.कृ.वि.स मान्यता (GKVS Approval)
6.35% Verified — CRITICAL

Agriculture Officer visits village → convenes Gram Sabha → GKVS committee formed (1,559 enrolled) → scheme awareness conducted → ग्रा.कृ.वि.स मान्यता GKVS approval initiated. Only 99 of 1,559 GKVSs verified. Kalamb (0/362), Washi (0/17), Omerga (0/148) at 0%.

🔴
Action: Emergency 30-day verification drive. 2 AAO officers per taluka. Target: 50 verifications/day.
🔍 2. Site Inspection — सहायक कृषी अधिकारी स्थळ पाहणी डेस्क -२
3,067 done

Asst. Agriculture Officer visits farmer's field → inspects landholding & crop pattern → completes सहायक कृषी अधिकारी स्थळ पाहणी डेस्क -२ site inspection → file forwarded to तालुका कृषी अधिकारी पूर्वसंमती डेस्क ३. 3,067 site inspections completed. Dharashiv leads (642), Kalamb (534).

✅
Strength: Site inspection stage is the pipeline's strongest point. Maintain pace.
💧 3. Irrigation — तालुका कृषी अधिकारी पूर्वसंमती डेस्क ३ to Installation
1,002 at सहायक कृषी अधिकारी स्थळ पाहणी डेस्क -२

Taluka Agriculture Officer issues pre-sanction (तालुका कृषी अधिकारी पूर्वसंमती डेस्क ३, 351 done) → farmer receives DBT subsidy → vendor installs drip/sprinkler → supervised by Circle AO. Dharashiv leads: 88 Drip + 226 Sprinkler = 314 total works.

⚠️
Monitor: Sprinkler 551 leads Drip 451. Drip awareness for water-stressed talukas like Bhum (18) and Washi (7).

Only 3.49% — 476 paid
